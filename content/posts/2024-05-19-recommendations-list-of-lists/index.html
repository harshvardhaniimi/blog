Listen

Podcasts

The Empire podcast covers the British empire, Roman empire, Ashoka’s empire, etc., and their impact on India and Asia. The episodes are phenomenal, detailed, and every episode covers aspects of history that I had no clue about! I’ve written about Warren Hastings and Robert Clive based on what I listened here. I’d recommend the first seven episodes in a row about the East India Company, 1857 rebellion, Gandhi3, Jinnah (Founding Father of Pakistan), Mountbatten (Last Viceroy of India), and Partition of India and birth of Pakistan.
